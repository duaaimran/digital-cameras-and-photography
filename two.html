Gallery

Welcome to the gallery! Here you can see some of the nostalgic pictures that I have taken with a point and shoot digital camera. I usually take pictures of my friends and family, as well as the art that I see around me. Enjoy!

I use a 2010 Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W310, a camera that my parents used to take pictures of me and my brother when we were kids. It has a 7.2 megapixel sensor and a 3x optical zoom lens. It is a simple camera that is easy to use and has a nostalgic feel to it.
